My Portfolio

Community Science Museum

Semester Project 1

This is the first site I made while studying at Noroff. Our task was to make a website for a community science museum where the target audience is primary and middle school children and families with young children. It's created solely with HTML and CSS.

Rainy Days

Cross Course Project 1

For this project we were tasked to make make an eCommerce site with HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript. We added our products to WordPress and fetched them through API. I chose the Rainy Days brief to make an online store for rain jackets.

Voyaging North

Project Exam 1

For our exam the first year we were to create a blog, using everything we're learned throughout the year. It's made with HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript. The blogs are fetched with a WP API and chronicles a boat trip I went on along the coast from the south of Norway, all the way north
